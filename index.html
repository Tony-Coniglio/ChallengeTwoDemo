About Me

My name is Tony Coniglio. I was born and raised in Austin, Texas. I have a BA in History from St. Edward's university in Austin, Texas, and a MA in History from Texas State University in San Marcos, Texas. I worked for 10 years in the petro-chemical industry specifically in print-grade films and polymers. While working in polymers I held positions ranging from intern and janitor to sales, procurement, and logistics. I have also worked in interior design, with a specific focus on logistics and white-glove services. During my time in interior design I founded and grew an in-house white-glove moving and delivery service as a new entity/sister company.
